Hi — I'm Whitney

The short version.

Licensed Professional Counselor (MA, LPC) practicing in Denver since 2012. Argosy-trained, Colorado-licensed, fourteen years in the room with adults, couples, and families. Currently online only, available across Colorado.

My approach is warm, direct, and trauma-informed — the kind of therapist who'll sit with you in the hard part and also tell you when it's time to get up. I draw from CBT, DBT, Solution-Focused, and Person-Centered work: practical tools first, deep self-awareness right behind them.

I work with anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, relationships, grief, parenting, and the postpartum stretch nobody warned you would feel like this. No clipboards. No worksheets that look like the SAT. Just real conversation toward the version of yourself you keep almost reaching.

How this works.

Reach out · Get to it · Keep going

i.

The first call

  • Fifteen minutes, free, no pitch deck and no pressure
  • You tell me what's loud, what's stuck, and what you've already tried
  • I tell you honestly whether I'm the right person for this — or who is
ii.

The actual work

  • Weekly, biweekly, or monthly — whatever fits your real life
  • Online sessions across Colorado, scheduled around your week
  • Goals written in plain language and revisited like they matter
iii.

The keep-going

  • Every 8–12 weeks we zoom out and check the map against the territory
  • Recalibrate when life rearranges itself, because it always does
  • Graduate when you're ready, with a kit you actually know how to use
